Overview

Designing for every week of the journey.

Pregnancy is one of the most transformative — and overwhelming — experiences in a person's life. Existing apps tend to be clinical and information-dense, failing to create an emotional connection with their users.

MomCare+ was designed from the ground up to feel like a caring companion. Warm, clear, and delightful. Each week unlocks new content about baby development, nutrition tips, exercises, and appointments — all in one place.

Feature 01

Weekly Baby Development Tracker

Each week, mothers see exactly how their baby is growing — compared to a familiar fruit, with precise size and weight metrics, and a curated list of developmental milestones. Quick-glance trackers anchor the bottom for daily essentials.

  • Pink-to-purple gradient hero with current week & trimester
  • Baby size comparison with real fruit imagery
  • Boy & Ağırlık metrics — 30 cm · 590 g
  • Weekly development milestones
  • Quick-access trackers: kicks, contractions, water, sleep
Feature 02

Appointment Management

Never miss a check-up. The appointments screen gives a clear view of upcoming visits, doctor details, and easy one-tap calling — all in a warm, readable layout.

  • Next appointment hero card with gradient background
  • Tabbed view: Upcoming / Past / My Doctors
  • Doctor profile with specialty and visit details
  • One-tap call and detail view actions
  • Location and time at a glance
Feature 03

Exercise & Nutrition Plans

Personalized daily plans for staying active and eating well. Exercise flows are categorized by type and difficulty; nutrition tracks calories, protein, water and supplements.

  • Daily activity progress card (3/8 exercises)
  • Physical / Mental / Breathing exercise tabs
  • Pregnancy-safe workout cards with duration badges
  • Nutrition dashboard: Kalori, Protein, Su, Folik Asit
  • Weekly meal plan with calorie targets per meal
Feature 04

Checklist & Baby Wishlist

Two powerful planning tools: a trimester-based checklist to track 39 pregnancy milestones, and a shareable baby product registry for family and friends.

  • Progress tracking: 12/39 tasks, 31% complete
  • Collapsible trimester sections
  • Baby registry with priority tags and price totals
  • Category filters: Baby Odası, Besleme, Bez…
  • Share link for family & friends
